Head

Prof. Pablo Balenzuela

Independent researcher

pbalen@df.uba.ar

In contemporary digital ecosystems, news and their repercussions propagate almost instantaneously. Our research aims to characterize the spatio-temporal patterns that emerge from these information flows. To this end, we analyze large-scale datasets from social media and news outlets, and develop mathematical frameworks that explain collective behavior based on elementary interactions among individuals.

Our theoretical work draws on statistical mechanics, nonlinear dynamics, and complex network theory, while our empirical methodology incorporates a broad suite of machine learning algorithms for data processing and analysis.
